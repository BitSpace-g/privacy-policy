Just Stack ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile game.

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, we use third-party services (e.g., Google AdMob) that may collect certain data automatically, such as:

  • Usage data (e.g., ad clicks, impressions)

2. Purpose of Collection

The information collected via third-party services is used solely for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Analyzing ad performance
  • Supporting app monetization

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Their privacy policy can be found at:

https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. User Control

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
